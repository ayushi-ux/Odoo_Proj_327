7-Day Diet Plans

Day 1

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with berries and a sprinkle of chia seeds

Snack: A small apple

Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and balsamic vinaigrette

Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus

Dinner: Baked salmon with steamed broccoli and quinoa

Day 2

Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, almond milk, and protein powder

Snack: Handful of almonds

Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap in a whole wheat tortilla

Snack: Celery sticks with peanut butter

Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables and brown rice

Day 3

Breakfast: Oatmeal with fresh fruit and a drizzle of honey

Snack: Greek yogurt

Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, and lemon dressing

Snack: Mixed nuts

Dinner: Grilled shrimp with a side of asparagus and a small sweet potato

Day 4

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole wheat toast

Snack: A small pear

Lunch: Chicken and vegetable soup with a side salad

Snack: Greek yogurt with a handful of berries

Dinner: Baked cod with roasted Brussels sprouts and quinoa

Day 5

Breakfast: Smoothie with kale, mango, and almond milk

Snack: Mixed nuts

Lunch: Turkey lettuce wraps with hummus and sliced veggies

Snack: Apple slices with almond butter

Dinner: Grilled chicken with a side of brown rice and green beans

Day 6

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with granola and honey

Snack: Celery sticks with hummus

Lunch: Shrimp and avocado salad with mixed greens and lemon vinaigrette

Snack: Handful of almonds

Dinner: Stir-fried chicken with broccoli and brown rice

Day 7

Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and a poached egg

Snack: Greek yogurt with berries

Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and balsamic vinaigrette

Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus

Dinner: Baked salmon with steamed broccoli and quinoa
